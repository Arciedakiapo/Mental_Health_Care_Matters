What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. In the Philippines, it's often overlooked due to stigma and lack of access. But we are working to change that.

Common Mental Health Issues

  • Depression: Affects 3.3 million Filipinos according to WHO.
  • Anxiety: Common among youth and working adults.
  • Suicide Prevention: Suicide is the second leading cause of death among 15–29-year-olds.
  • Trauma & PTSD: Especially among typhoon, earthquake, and conflict survivors.

Philippine Mental Health Resources
